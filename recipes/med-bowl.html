Mediterranean Bowl

Description

This dish delevers a pleasant fresh flavor with it cucumbers and sauce!

Ingredients

For this dish you will need:

  • Chickpeas
  • Cucumbers
  • Artichoke Hearts
  • Rice

Step by Step:

  1. Bake chickpeas until soft
  2. Make rice
  3. Bake artichoke hearts
  4. Combine and enjoy!
